## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
Yes, completely. SpendWise AI is a free tool from Credex. We make money when high-savings teams choose to work with us for discounted AI credits — and we only ask about that after we've shown you your results.

**Q: Do I need to connect my accounts or billing dashboards?**
No. You enter your tools, plans, and spend manually. It takes about 2 minutes. We don't have access to your card or your accounts.

**Q: How current is the pricing data?**
Every number in the audit engine traces to an official vendor pricing page, verified the week of our last update. Check PRICING_DATA.md in our public GitHub repo for every source with verification date.

**Q: What happens to my data?**
The audit result (tools and savings) is stored with a public-shareable URL. Your email — if you choose to share it — is stored separately and never included in the public URL. We don't sell your data.

**Q: What is Credex and why did they build this?**
Credex sources discounted AI infrastructure credits from companies that overforecast or pivoted. This audit tool helps startups see where they're overspending — and for teams saving $500+/month, Credex is often the next step. The tool is genuinely useful regardless of whether you ever work with us.
